FAQs

Do you have more questions? Book a call .

Why wouldn 't I just hire a full-time developer?

It 's surprisingly difficult to find good developers, not to mention developers with experience in building MVPs.

It 's even more difficult to find a developer that can do architecture, frontend, backend, cloud infrastructure and DevOps work. And you 'll need all this to launch your MVP. In most cases, you 'll probably have to hire multiple people, which will increase your costs considerably.

Who are the developers?

It 's just me, Cata. I 've spent the last 20+ years architecting and building web applications.

What stack or technologies do you work with?

Unless the project requires a specific tech stack, our default stack is composed of: Node / Feathers / Sequelize / Postgres (for backend), React / Next (frontend) and Terraform / Terragrunt for cloud infrastructure.

How do I make requests?

Once you signup, you 'll get a dedicated Slack channel where you can make as many requests as you want. We 'll break big or complex requests into smaller ones that we can work on. Requests will be handled one at a time.

How fast will I get results?

On average, most requests are delivered in 2 days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.

What if I need to make changes?

No worries! We 'll continue to revise our solution until you 're 100% satisfied.

Why do you price your work this way?

We think this is the best way for you to get your MVP done: flat and stress-free pricing, super fast delivery, and a world-class developer as an extension (or the entirety) of your team, without having to hire one.

How does the pause feature work?

We understand you may not have enough dev work to fill up entire month. Perhaps you only have one or two quick requests at the moment. That 's where pausing your subscription comes in handy. Billing cycles are based on 31 day period. Let 's say you sign up and use the service for 21 days, and then decide to pause your subscription. This means that the billing cycle will be paused and you 'll have 10 days of service remaining to be used anytime in the future.

Are there any refunds?

Yes. Trying a new service can be intimidating. That 's why there 's a 30 day money-back guarantee, so you can feel totally safe subscribing.
